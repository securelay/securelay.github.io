Securelay is an open-source, feature-rich, secure and ephemeral http-relay for small data, with a REST API. Read full specifications here.

The securelay organization, as a non-profit, hosts public endpoint(s) for non-commercial, individual use and trial or testing purposes. No payment, credit-card details or even email ID is required for using those endpoints.

If you want to use Securelay for commercial purposes, please host your own instance using popular cloud hosting providers or your own server(s). You are welcome to use our open-source codebase and guides, or contact us for help and queries.

Project Release Status: Perpetual Beta

Note: The public endpoints are maintained non-commercially through sponsorships. This service, being free, is, as yet, not scalable. PLEASE, DO NOT ABUSE OR MISUSE!
